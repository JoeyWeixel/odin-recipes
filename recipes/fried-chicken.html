Fried Chicken

Description

Crispy fried chicken with a little bit of spice

Ingredients

  • Peanut Oil
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Flour
  • Corn Starch
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pickle Juice
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Oregano
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne Pepper Powder
  • Buttermilk

Steps

  1. Cut 2 chicken breasts horizontally to produce 4 pieces
  2. Add 1/2 cup pickle juice to a 1 gallon plastic bag
  3. Add 1 teaspoon oregano, and salt, and 2 teaspoons garlic powder, onion poder, pepper, paprika, and cayenne pepper powder to the bag
  4. Place chicken in the bag, massage mixture into the chicken and refrigerate for at least 2 hours
  5. Mix 1 and 1/2 cup buttermilk, 1 egg and a pinch of salt in a small bowl
  6. Combine 1 and 1/2 cups flour, 1/2 cup cornstarch and a pinch of salt in a bowl
  7. Dip the chicken in the buttermilk mixture and then dredge in the flour mixture twice
  8. Add about 1 inch of peanut oil to a cast iron skillet on medium heat
  9. Gently place chicken into the oil and wait 2 minuts before turning over
  10. Take out once the chicken is golden brown
