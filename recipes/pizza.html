Pizza

Description

A dish consisting of a circular bread base with a crispy crust, red tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings of choice.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Spread pizza dough into a thin circle, roll edges slightly to create crust
  3. Spread tomoato sauce onto the dough, apply amount to taste
  4. Sprinkle cheese liberally onto the sauce
  5. Add desired toppings
  6. Bake until golden brown
