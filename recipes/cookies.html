Cookies

Description

This recipe will allow you to bake delicious chocolate chip cookies from scratch in your very own oven!

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • two large eggs
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Combine flour, salt, and baking soda in small bowl
  3. Beat granulated sugar, brown sugar, butter, and vanilla extract in large bowl
  4. Add eggs to the large mixing bowl, one at a time, beating well after each addition
  5. Gradually beat in dry mixture
  6. Stir in chocolate chips
  7. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until golden brown
