Peanut Butter Banana Toast

Description

A light meal for breakfast or a quick snack during the day. Healthy enough for an everyday meal

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread of choice
  • Peanut butter
  • Ripe Banana

Steps

  1. Toast your bread until golden brown
  2. Spead peanut butter on one side of toast
  3. Cut banana into approxamitely 1/2 inch slices and place on toast
